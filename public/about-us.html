About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Modern Hardwares, your trusted partner for high-quality paints, Plywoods, wall stickers, hinges, locks, handles, and other accessories. Our journey began five years ago with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide top-notch products that enhance the beauty and functionality of homes and businesses.

We are a family-run business, founded and operated by myself and my three cousins. As a middle-class family, we understand the importance of quality and affordability. Our combined efforts and passion have allowed us to grow and serve our community with dedication and integrity..

Our Mission

At Modern Hardwares, our mission is to bring innovative and reliable products to the market. We are constantly exploring new trends and technologies to ensure our offerings meet the highest standards. Whether you're looking to revamp your living space with vibrant paints or secure your home with sturdy locks and handles, we have something for everyone.
